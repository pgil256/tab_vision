## 4. WS2 (cross-string axis from the nut OBB) â€” measured negative, deferred

The neck OBB the homography fits from mis-estimates the string span at the nut: the
nut OBB's measured cross-string width is **14â€“29% narrower** (ratio 0.71â€“0.86) and
its center is **offset** (0.15â€“1.09 neck-edge-lengths; 294 is a full edge off). So
there is real geometric signal. A first cut re-fits the homography with the
cross-string axis anchored to the nut OBB (`predictions_to_homography_nut_axis` /
`calibrate_board`, opt-in `--calibrate-board`).

**Result: net-negative and inconsistent â€” leading 0.547 < WS1 0.574.** It helps the
big nut-only clips (235 0.419â†’0.435, 179 0.598â†’0.609, 212 0.835â†’0.848) but hurts the
fret-rich ones (104 0.619â†’0.468, 142 0.526â†’0.464, 027) and 294 (0.584â†’0.541, a
misdetected nut). The full homography re-fit **perturbs the x-axis WS1 already got
right**, and the nut OBB is itself noisy. The design pairs the cross-string axis
with **geometry-aware confidence** precisely for this reason: without a quality gate
to suppress bad re-fits, the noisy ones drag down the good ones. WS2 is deferred
behind that gate (a fret-richness routing + nut-plausibility / reprojection-
consistency selector); kept opt-in (default off) so it does not affect WS1.

Asked whether to thread WS1's nonlinear fret-map through the Â§8 contract to
production + recalibrate the gate (WS5) to *materialise* the lift, we measured the
prerequisite first: does WS1's string resolution beat the **audio playability
prior** on the contested notes? It does not.

The audio prior is **~0.20 better** than the video chain. Fusion adds the video
term to the audio emission cost, so any non-trivial `lambda_vision` pulls the
decision toward the *worse* source and degrades Tab F1; a tiny weight is a no-op.
**No gate yields a net-positive lift** â€” the gate's best move is to keep video out
(which the 0.71 gate already does). So "make WS1 real" would thread better geometry
to production for **zero headline gain**; we did **not** change the immutable Â§8
contract for it (DECISIONS 2026-06-29).

This reframes chunk-6: on in-the-wild GAPS the ordering is **audio 0.778 >
geometry-video 0.574 > learned-video (worse)** â€” video only "wins" via the *oracle*
(0.973), which no real chain approaches. String-resolution video is not an additive
lever over the audio prior on this corpus; the single-line **video** target

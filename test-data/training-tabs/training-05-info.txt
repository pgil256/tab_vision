Title: Octave Shapes Across the Neck
Category: Position Ambiguity
BPM: 72
Duration: ~20 seconds
Tests: Octave pairs (same note, different octave) played on adjacent string
groups. E.g., E2 on low E open + E3 on D string fret 2. This targets the
known octave harmonic filter issue - the system must NOT filter real octaves
as harmonics. Also tests whether 2-note "chords" get correct positions.

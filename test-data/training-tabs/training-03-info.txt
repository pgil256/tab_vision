Title: Same Pitch, Three Positions
Category: Position Ambiguity
Instrument: electric
BPM: 66
Duration: ~25 seconds
Tests: Notes that can be played at 3 different string/fret combos.
For example, the note A4 (MIDI 69) can be played at e-string fret 5,
B-string fret 10, or G-string fret 14. Plays each option with pauses.
Extremely hard for audio-only; video hand position is the tiebreaker.

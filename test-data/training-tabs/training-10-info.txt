Title: Mixed Chord Types - Open to Barre Transitions
Category: Chord Varieties
BPM: 84
Duration: ~20 seconds
Tests: Transitioning between open chords and barre chords in the same
progression. Am (open) - F (barre) - C (open) - G (open) - Bb (barre) -
Am (open). Tests the system's ability to handle anchor switching between
open position and barred positions within a single sequence.

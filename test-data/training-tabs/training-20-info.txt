Title: Open Strings and Muted Notes
Category: Edge Cases
Instrument: acoustic or electric
BPM: 72
Duration: ~20 seconds
Tests: Mix of open strings (fret 0) and muted/dead notes (X). Open strings
are common in real playing but have no finger on the fretboard - the video
pipeline must recognize the absence of a finger. Muted notes produce a
percussive sound with no pitch. Both are edge cases for the fusion engine.
Pattern: play open string, play fretted note, play muted strum.

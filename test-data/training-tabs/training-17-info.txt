Title: High Fret Melody (Frets 12-17)
Category: Edge Cases
BPM: 72
Duration: ~20 seconds
Tests: Melody played entirely above the 12th fret on strings 1-3.
This is difficult for the video pipeline because frets are closer together
at the high end, making finger position harder to resolve. Also tests
whether the system handles 2-digit fret numbers correctly in tabs.

Title: String Skipping Melody
Category: Single-Note Passages
Instrument: acoustic or electric
BPM: 84
Duration: ~20 seconds
Tests: Single notes that skip strings (e.g., low E then G, then B, then D).
This is NOT scalar motion - the intervals are wide and unpredictable.
Tests whether the system correctly handles non-adjacent string assignments
without the melodic segment correction incorrectly "fixing" them onto
one string.

Title: Hammer-Ons and Pull-Offs
Category: Edge Cases
BPM: 90
Duration: ~20 seconds
Tests: Legato passages with hammer-ons (h) and pull-offs (p). The audio
will detect all notes but hammer-ons/pull-offs have different attack
characteristics than picked notes (softer onset). Tests whether the pitch
detector catches these weaker onsets. Play each hammer-on and pull-off
with enough force to be heard clearly.
Note: Tab notation uses h and p but ground truth only tracks fret numbers.

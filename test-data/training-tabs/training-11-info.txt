Title: Pentatonic Scale - Box 1 (Am)
Category: Single-Note Passages
Instrument: acoustic or electric
BPM: 90
Duration: ~15 seconds
Tests: A minor pentatonic in the classic box 1 position (frets 5-8).
All notes on one position, ascending then descending. This is a
fundamental pattern that should produce very high accuracy. Tests
whether the melodic segment correction works properly on a single-box
scale run.

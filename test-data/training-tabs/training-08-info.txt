Title: Power Chords - Root 6 and Root 5
Category: Chord Varieties
Instrument: acoustic or electric
BPM: 100
Duration: ~15 seconds
Tests: 2-note and 3-note power chords (root + fifth, optionally + octave).
Played on low E root and A root. These are partial chords (not all 6 strings)
so tests the chord size limiting and muted string detection. Mute unused
strings with fretting hand. Faster tempo tests detection under quicker changes.

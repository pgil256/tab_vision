Title: Chromatic Exercise - All Frets 1-4
Category: Single-Note Passages
Instrument: acoustic or electric
BPM: 80
Duration: ~25 seconds
Tests: 1-2-3-4 chromatic exercise on each string, low E through high e.
This is the classic finger independence exercise. Every fret from 1-4 on
every string. Tests accurate detection of half-step intervals and
string-by-string tracking. Known difficulty: cross-string transitions
can confuse the slide correction algorithm.

Title: Barre Chord Progression
Category: Chord Varieties
Instrument: acoustic or electric
BPM: 72
Duration: ~20 seconds
Tests: Full 6-string barre chords at various positions. F major (fret 1),
Bb major (fret 1 A-shape), B minor (fret 2), F# minor (fret 2 E-shape).
No open strings at all. Tests whether the system handles barre positions
where multiple strings share the same fret (the barre finger).

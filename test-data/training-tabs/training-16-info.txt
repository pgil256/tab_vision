Title: Wide Position Jumps
Category: Edge Cases
Instrument: electric
BPM: 66
Duration: ~25 seconds
Tests: Notes that jump from low frets to high frets within a few beats.
Fret 2 -> fret 12, fret 1 -> fret 9, etc. Tests the hand position anchor
system's ability to handle abrupt shifts. The slide correction algorithm
must NOT merge these into slides - they are discrete jumps.
Play each note cleanly with a brief pause for the hand shift.
